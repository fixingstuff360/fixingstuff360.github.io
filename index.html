FAQ

Reset Glitch Hack (RGH) is a hardware modification which allows you to run unsigned code, mods, game backups, and homebrew.

The process generally takes a few days, depending on the method and shipping times.

I use Tracked 48, so usually 2-3 days; however Tracked 24 can be arranged during your purpose.

Customers pay postage on a send-in, but I pay postage when they purchase one of my consoles. Postage is around £6.70 one way.

Firstly, do NOT buy a fake one as they are bound to have issues and are made with cheap materials, which will not make them last as long. Sometimes I have power supplies in stock, so ask me for one. If I don’t have any, I will direct you to an eBay listing from a seller I trust.

I don't Provide a warranty on consoles in usual scenario but if I do you will be informed.
